Our Story

About The Eden Festival

The Eden Festival was founded in 2024 by Julia Torgovitskaya Levitan, the founder and CEO of Cadenza Artists. It produces innovative and transformative, world-class, multi-genre performances while cultivating future generations of artist-leaders through immersive, long-term training and a summer intensive.

The festival commissions bold artistic works that encourage people to approach the world's pressing problems with curiosity, creativity, and invigorated imagination. We are providing an essential beacon of connection and inspiration for individuals, families, and communities of all ages, at a time when our culture is struggling to find its center.

Our Invitation

Awaken the senses and fire up the human imagination

We want to awaken the senses and fire up the human imagination! At The Eden Festival, we explore big questions. We want you to emerge from your experiences at the Festival feeling greater possibility and creativity, better equipped and willing to explore complexity from a fresh perspective.

Inspired to imagine differently

We are inspired by Krista Tippett's call to action in her On Being project:

“We envision a world that is more fluent in its own humanity — and thus able to rise to the great challenges and promise of this century.”

Krista Tippett, On Being

Guided by community

The arts as a bridge to shared humanity

The arts are a powerful vehicle to inspire creativity, connect people across places and cultures, and bring out our shared humanity.

An interdependent world

We envision an interdependent world in which people and communities navigate uncertainty with creativity; a world in which people draw on the arts and nature to cultivate humanity and understanding, thereby expanding possibility.

The Eden Festival will be a leader in creating and exporting these artistic experiences globally. Join us in creating together a brighter vision and intention for our shared human journey.

A capital campaign for bold venues

The Eden Festival has recently launched a $120 Million capital campaign to raise funding for the state of the art venues to be built ahead of the upcoming 2034 Winter Olympic Games in a scenic mountain enclave near Eden, Utah, just one hour north of Salt Lake City.

Immersive experiences in nature

Located directly between the mountainside towns of Eden and Paradise, and with satellite events across Northern and Southern Utah, The Eden Festival will be a destination for those who want to experience extraordinary, bold artistic experiences against a backdrop of pristine 360 degree views of the Utah mountains and the stunning Pineview Reservoir.

The festival will offer multi-sensory experiences that pair music with culinary adventures and artistic explorations across medium and genre to embrace a holistic aesthetic that celebrates the spectacular beauty of its natural environment.

Celebrating collaborative art-making and shared imagination along the Boise riverfront.
