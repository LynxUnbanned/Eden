Semiquincentennial Spotlight

Celebrating 250 Years of Imagination & Unity

As the United States marks its 250th anniversary, The Eden Festival is orchestrating a year of collaborations that honor the nation's creative spirit—bridging artists, communities, and the stories that define our future.

Explore Festival Highlights View the 250th timeline

Honoring the Past, Designing the Future

A living canvas of American ingenuity

From riverside installations to nationwide workshops, we're weaving the threads of the American story into immersive experiences that champion diversity, resilience, and collective imagination.

Voices of the 50

A collaborative series pairing artists from every state with local youth ensembles to tell stories rooted in place and possibility.

River of Light

An illuminated procession along the Boise riverfront inspired by the evolving American flag, featuring choreographed drone art.

Future Builders Lab

Innovation labs where communities design civic art prototypes that imagine the next 250 years of shared cultural spaces.

National Tour

Milestones on the road to 2026

  1. 2024

    Semiquincentennial announcement

    The Eden Festival commits to a multi-year partnership with national arts organizations to co-create a flagship celebration.

  2. 2025

    Community resonance residencies

    Artists-in-residence embed within five regions, gathering stories and crafting performances that reflect local histories.

  3. 2026

    Semiquincentennial culmination

    A national broadcast event and in-person festival featuring collaborative premieres, participatory installations, and civic dialogues.
